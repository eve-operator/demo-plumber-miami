## SMS Campaign Research
- **First message response rate:** 9.3%
- **First follow-up:** 4.8%
- **Second follow-up:** 2.9%
- **Third:** 1.5%
- **Key insight:** First message has to land the reply - diminishing returns are steep
- **Best practice:** Short, personal, reference their business specifically
- **Multi-channel:** SMS + call combo boosts answer rate by ~23%

### Winning Message Principles
1. Keep under 150 characters
2. Personalize with business name
3. Offer value, not just "buy my service"
4. Make response frictionless (yes/no questions)
5. Follow up within 24-48 hours if no response

## Message Templates (Test variants)
### Option A (Short & Direct)
```
Hi [Name], I noticed [Business] doesn't have a website yet. I help Miami small businesses get found online. Quick call to see if it makes sense? - [Your Name]
```

### Option B (Value-First)
```
Hi [Name], I'm reaching out because I saw [Business] has great reviews but no website. Many of your competitors are getting customers online - want me to show you how? No pressure. - [Your Name]
```

### Option C (Modern/casual)
```
Hey [Business] 👋 No website? You're missing out on customers searching for you on Google right now. I build affordable sites for Miami businesses - happy to show you what you're missing.
```

### Option D (Question-based - TEST)
```
Does [Business] show up when people Google for [Category] services in Miami? Happy to show you free of charge.
```
