# Revenue Strategy - Warm Leads Focus

## Core Strategy: Find People Asking For Help

Instead of cold outreach, find people who ALREADY posted asking for help with websites/development.

## Where to Find Warm Leads

### 1. Reddit (via Brave Search - works)
Search queries that find people asking for help:
- `site:reddit.com r/smallbusiness "need a website"`
- `site:reddit.com r/webdev "looking for developer"`  
- `site:reddit.com r/freelance "need help with website"`

### 2. IndieHackers
- People building products often need dev help
- Can browse products, find founders

### 3. HackerNews Hiring
- Monthly "Who is hiring" threads
- Companies hiring for web roles

### 4. Direct Company Careers Pages
- Shopify, Cloudflare, Canonical, Automattic, GitLab
- All have direct apply forms that work

## What Works Now
- ✅ Brave Search - can find warm leads
- ✅ Gmail - can send outreach emails
- ✅ Job ATS forms (Lever, Breezy) - can fill without login

## What I'm Executing

1. **Search for "need a website" posts** on Reddit via Brave
2. **Email them directly** (max 5-10/day, personalized)
3. **Apply to company careers directly** (Shopify, Cloudflare, etc.)
