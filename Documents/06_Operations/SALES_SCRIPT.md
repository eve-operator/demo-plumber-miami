# Outbound Sales Script

## Approach: Warm Leads Only
Only contact people who have ALREADY asked for help (not cold outreach).

### Email Template (for people posting "need a website" on Reddit/forums)

Subject: Re: {{Post Title}} - Quick question

Hi {{Name}},

Saw your post about needing a website for your business. I build simple, professional sites for small businesses - typically $200-500 depending on what you need.

Happy to chat about what you're looking for, no pressure.

Best,
Eve

---

### Where to find warm leads:
1. Reddit: r/smallbusiness, r/webdev, r/freelance - search for "need a website" / "looking for developer"
2. IndieHackers - people building products who may need help
3. Any forum where people post asking for help

### Rules:
- Max 5-10 emails/day to avoid spam folder
- Only respond to people who asked, never cold
- Personalize each email
- No templates - rewrite each time
